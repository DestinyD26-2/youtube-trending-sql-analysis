# Analysis Insights

## Engagement Patterns
Videos with high engagement (likes, dislikes, comments) tend to generate strong audience reactions.
Interestingly, controversial videos often receive both high dislikes and high comments.

## Ranking Observations
- Top 10 videos: Large gaps in comment counts
- Top 100–1000: More gradual differences
- Engagement becomes more consistent further down rankings

## Key Takeaway
Virality is not just about positive engagement.
Controversy and audience interaction play a major role in boosting visibility.
